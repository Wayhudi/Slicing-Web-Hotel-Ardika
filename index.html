Welcome to

Freddies Resort & Villas Ubud

Freddies Resort & Villas Ubud is designed as a private and peaceful sanctuary, offering a unique blend of both 'in-villa' and 'resort-style' experiences. Perfect for the discerning traveler, our villas provide exceptional privacy, personalized service, and meticulous attention to detail. Ideally located near Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali, our villas are a short distance from popular attractions, art galleries, and cultural landmarks. Enjoy a luxurious and tranquil stay with the perfect balance of serenity and easy access to the vibrant Ubud art and culture scene.
